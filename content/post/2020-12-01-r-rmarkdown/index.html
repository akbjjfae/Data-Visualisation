Australia covers an area of 7.692 million square kilometers and has a population of only 25.69 million. Of the 25.69 million people, 37.13 per cent identified themselves as Australians (of which 2.7 per cent were of original Aboriginal descent); 31.65% of Australians of English origin; Irish-Australian accounted for 9.08%; Scottish Australians make up 7.56%; Italian-Australian accounted for 4.29%; German-Australian 4.09%; 3.37% are Chinese; 1.84% are of Greek origin.
